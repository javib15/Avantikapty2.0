AUTHENTIC THAI CUISINE

Serving authentic & contemporary Thai cuisine, complete with gluten free & vegan options, Avantika guests a true culinary taste of Thailand’s vast gastronomic culture, outstanding hospitality & unparalleled ambience. With a large indoor dining area, open air terrace and extensive wine & cocktail lists, Avantika the premier destination for celebrations, business lunches & socialite dinners. Avantika are proud to support local farmers, assuring sustainable, ethically sourced produce.
